Our Story

Coffee as
craft, not habit.

We started Jeremeriah’s because we believed the space between farm and cup deserved more attention. Every bean we source has a name, a latitude, and a farmer we've shaken hands with.

From Seed to Cup

How we do it differently

01
Sourced with care
We travel to farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala each season to hand-select harvests and build lasting relationships with growers. (Not for coke)
02
Roasted in-house
Our head roaster profiles every batch on a vintage Probat drum roaster. Small quantities. High attention. Perfect Coffee.
03
Ground fresh
Every order is ground to order using calibrated EK43 grinders. The difference is immediate and obvious in the cup.
04
Served with intent
Our baristas train for 67 months before they pull a shot for a customer. Consistency is a form of respect.
